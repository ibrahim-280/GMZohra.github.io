About Factory

GM ZOHRA

A forward-thinking and design-focused company, collaborates with renowned global brands, organizations, and entrepreneurs. Our mission is to translate marketing and business objectives into innovative design strategies, resulting in successful new products. From our humble beginnings of producing a thousand balls per day, we have evolved into a global supplier of premium sports goods. Our success is largely attributed to the exceptional craftsmanship of our local workers, who work their magic with their skilled hands.
While we now employ sophisticated technology and intelligent machinery to meet international standards, we remain rooted in the legacy of our earliest workers. Their expertise laid the foundation for our industry. Despite technological advancements, we recognize that nothing compares to the unmatched skill of our workers. This skill is a major draw for exporters, sellers, and suppliers.
Our commitment lies in meeting user needs and marketplace demands. We continuously strive to provide solutions that bring value to our clients and their customers. Our ultimate goal is to drive positive change, transform lives, and promote well-being while maintaining environmental consciousness. We take immense pride in our in-house skills enhancement programs and partnerships with charities and non-profit organizations. Together, we tackle challenges in the developing world, turning them into opportunities for growth and progress.
